# Soterius Vision

---

## Mission

Help professional services firms understand, measure and reduce digital risk.

---

## Current Positioning

Security Rating Platform for Professional Services Firms.

---

## Future Positioning

Digital Risk Management Platform.

---

## Core Principles

- **Business-first communication** — lead with risk and impact, not technical findings
- **Risk before technology** — executives need to understand exposure, not CVE identifiers
- **Executive-friendly reporting** — every output must be readable by a managing partner
- **Continuous monitoring over one-time scanning** — a single scan is a snapshot; value comes from tracking change over time
- **Trust and credibility over technical complexity** — firms need a trusted partner, not another tool

---

## Product Evolution

### Phase 1 — Security Rating
Firms can scan their domain and receive a Security Rating (0–999) with a five-band risk classification, category breakdown, and actionable findings. Baseline established.

### Phase 2 — Security Rating + Monitoring
Firms can track their Security Rating over time. Score history, trend analysis, and change detection show whether posture is improving or deteriorating. Monitoring subscriptions enable automated rescans and alerts.

### Phase 3 — Digital Risk Management
A comprehensive platform for ongoing risk governance. Industry benchmarking, peer comparison, regulatory evidence packs (SRA, FCA, ICO), and a managed service offering for firms that need expert remediation.

---

## Target Customers

- Solicitors
- Accountants
- Financial Advisers
- Surveyors
- Professional Services Firms

These firms share common characteristics: they handle sensitive client data, operate under regulatory obligations, and lack the internal security expertise or budget for enterprise-grade tools.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Become the trusted digital risk platform used by professional services firms to understand their security posture, monitor change, reduce risk and demonstrate continuous improvement.
